Privacy Policy

Our Commitment To Privacy

Your privacy is important to us here at Morganti. We provide this notice explaining our online information practices and the choices you can make about the way your information is collected and used. Morganti, being an ecommerce service, acts as conduit for a large amount of sensitive and private data. We strive to maintain the privacy of both our merchant clients and the shoppers who patronize their online stores. It is our strict policy not to share, rent or sell such data to anyone without your electronic consent.

Information We Collect

Morganti collects personally identifiable information from our users at several different points on our websites, https://morgantiluxury.com

Information Use and Ownership

If we have trouble processing an order, we will use this personally identifiable information to contact you. We may also use this personally identifiable information to communicate with you for several other reasons. We may use your e-mail to send you newsletters and updates. Morganti is the sole owner of the personally identifiable information related to individuals, companies or entities purchasing services or products from us.

Promotional Communications

We will occasionally send you communications on products, services, special deals, promotions and news at Morganti. Out of respect for your privacy, we present the option not to receive these types of communications. Please see the “Choice and Opt-out” below.

Service-related Announcements

We will send you service-related announcements on rare occasions when it is necessary to do so, for instance, if our service is temporarily suspended for maintenance. You may not opt-out of these communications, which are not promotional in nature. If you do not wish to receive them, you have the option to cancel your account.

Customer Service

Based upon the personally identifiable information you provide us, we will send you a welcoming e-mail to verify your signup. We will also communicate with you in response to your inquiries, to provide the services you request, to invoice you and to manage your account. We will communicate with you by e-mail, telephone or mail.

Information Sharing and Disclosure

Aggregate Information (non-personally identifiable)

We may share aggregated demographic information about our user base with our marketing partners and advertisers. This information does not identify individual users. We do not link aggregate user data with personally identifiable information.

Legal Disclaimer

We reserve the right to disclose your personally identifiable information as required by law and when we believe that disclosure is necessary to protect our rights and/or to comply with a judicial proceeding, court order, or legal process served on us.

Choice/Opt-out

If you no longer wish to receive promotional communications, you may opt-out of receiving them by following the instructions included in each communication or by e-mailing us at contact@morgantiluxury.com

Business Transitions

In the event Morganti goes through a business transition, such as a merger, acquisition by another company, or sale of all or a portion of its assets, your personally identifiable information will likely be among the assets transferred. You will be notified via e-mail or prominent notice on our websites of any such change in ownership.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions regarding our privacy policy, please contact us at: contact@morgantiluxury.com
