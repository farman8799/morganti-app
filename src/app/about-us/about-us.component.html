Our Brand

Morganti Luxury is a Swiss-Italian family-owned brand that specializes in perfumery and luxury goods. Our focus is to bring together precious fragrances and inspiration to offer a fresh perspective on luxury.

Morganti Luxury Perfumes are meticulously crafted in Dubai, United Arab Emirates, a prominent player in the global perfume industry. Our dedication to creating exceptional fragrances is deeply rooted in our Swiss-Italian heritage. This heritage ensures that we maintain an unwavering commitment to using only the finest quality ingredients.

The trust and loyalty of our customers are highly valued by us. We strive to uphold our Swiss-Italian values and earn the continued trust of our customers through our unwavering dedication to excellence.

We strongly believe in the importance of giving back to nature. As part of our objective at Morganti Luxury, our aim is to regenerate and replenish what we take from nature. This ensures that each of our creations is not only unique but also sustainable.

Our Perfume

Morganti Luxury creates authentic signature fragrances of character for unique creative people.

Kilian Morganti has selected women and men with strong personality and sensibility to give birth to those amazing scents. His multi-cultural inspirations are also felt in his creative process.

Each scent is a result of a long-term workaround premier quality scents and creative fragrance structure.

Morganti Luxury refuses compromise and give life to objects that arouse mystics emotions.
